Hi I'm Lei Yang 杨蕾, a final year Ph.D. student in the Department of Astronomy at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I work with Prof. Yipeng Jing and Prof. Xiaohu Yang on improving measurement accuracy of galaxy clustering.
I graduated from Peking University in 2012 with Master degreen in Astrophysics. Before that, I graduated with a B.S. in Optical information and technology at Hebei University.
My primary research interest lies in observational cosmology, particular in galaxy-halo connection, galaxy formation and evolution, and large-scale structure of the universe.
